createNotifier offers a means to manually initiate signal re-computations through referencing the created notifier signal. Its functionality mirrors that of RxJS Subject. With each event, you can alert the subject through its next method. Likewise, employing createNotifier is convenient for manually instigating a "refresh" and responding to it in a signal-oriented manner.

To initiate the signal update, simply call refreshNotifier.notify(). Subsequently, you can prompt the re-computation of any computed or effect (including computedAsync from ngx-lift) by accessing the signal returned upon listening.

Initially, refreshNotifier.listen() returns 0. Whenever notify is explicitly called, the value adds 1.
