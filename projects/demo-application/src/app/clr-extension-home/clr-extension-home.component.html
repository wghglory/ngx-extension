The clr-extension library is designed to augment the capabilities of the Clarity library by providing a set of reusable components built on top of clarity components, and powerful collection of utilities, operators.

Features

Operators

clr-extension introduces powerful operators, such as dgState, designed to amplify the functionality of the Clarity library. The dgState operator streamlines datagrid state management, offering developers an intuitive and efficient approach when working with Clarity datagrids.

Utilities

Our library includes a suite of utilities finely tuned for Clarity components. These utilities simplify common tasks and operations, providing developers with a seamless and cohesive experience when interacting with Clarity components.

Components

clr-extension offers a variety of custom components adhering to Clarity styling standards, including toast, alert, and more.
